About

The Problem We're Solving

Every semester, thousands of campus students waste precious time standing in long canteen queues — under harsh summer sun or in monsoon downpours. Many simply skip meals because walking to the canteen feels too far.

CampusBite AI was built to eliminate this problem with a smart, affordable, campus-native food delivery system that runs entirely within your campus — with just one delivery partner and a brilliant AI layer.

Our model is sustainable: ₹10 delivery charge per order with ₹5 from every order going directly to campus development fund.
